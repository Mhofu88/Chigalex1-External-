📱 CHAPTER 2A — PRACTICAL PI WALLET TRAINING

Many pioneers across Africa are new to cryptocurrency payments and digital wallets.

This section simplifies how to safely use the Pi Wallet in practical everyday situations.

🟢 Understanding the Pi Wallet

The Pi Wallet is your personal digital wallet used to:

  • ✔ Store your migrated Pi safely
  • ✔ Send Pi to other pioneers
  • ✔ Receive Pi payments
  • ✔ Pay merchants accepting Pi
  • ✔ Participate in ecosystem transactions

Your wallet belongs to YOU.

Nobody controls your wallet except the person with access to the 24-word passphrase.

🔐 Understanding Your Wallet Address

Your wallet address works similarly to a bank account number.

People use your wallet address to send Pi to you.

Your wallet address is safe to share.

BUT NEVER SHARE YOUR 24-WORD PASSPHRASE.

📥 How to Receive Pi

  1. Open Pi Browser
  2. Open Pi Wallet
  3. Copy your wallet address
  4. Send the address to the sender
  5. Wait for transaction confirmation
  6. Check wallet balance update

📤 How to Send Pi

  1. Open Pi Wallet
  2. Click “Pay / Send”
  3. Paste receiver wallet address carefully
  4. Enter amount
  5. Double-check all details
  6. Confirm transaction

⚠️ Important Safety Rules

  • ✔ Always verify wallet addresses carefully
  • ✔ Never rush transactions
  • ✔ Start with small test amounts first
  • ✔ Never share your passphrase
  • ✔ Avoid fake support agents and scammers
  • ✔ Be careful with screenshots containing sensitive information

🏪 Paying Merchants Using Pi

When paying merchants:

  • ✔ Confirm the agreed amount first
  • ✔ Verify merchant wallet address
  • ✔ Send payment carefully
  • ✔ Wait for merchant confirmation
  • ✔ Keep transaction screenshots if necessary
